Artificial Intelligence in Economics | Fall 2024

Project Overview

This project analyzes the U.S. fashion industry through an economic and technological lens, focusing on employment trends and the impact of artificial intelligence on a mature industry.

Industry data are used to evaluate how firms adapt to efficiency-driven growth and digital transformation.

The analysis connects AI-enabled insights with broader economic implications and professional skill development.
