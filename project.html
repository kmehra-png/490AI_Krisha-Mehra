AI Class Project

AI in Economics | Fall 2025

Project Overview

This project analyzes the U.S. fashion industry to understand how artificial intelligence is reshaping a mature, highly globalized consumer sector. Using industry sales, trade, and employment indicators, it examines how AI adoption changes production efficiency, forecasting, and competitive strategy across the fashion value chain.

The project highlights long-term shifts such as declining domestic apparel manufacturing employment, growth in e-commerce, and the rise of data-driven merchandising and marketing. It also evaluates how AI tools support trend prediction, inventory planning, and personalization.

Finally, the analysis connects these industry changes to skill demands and career pathways, emphasizing how AI complements human judgment in marketing, strategy, and operations rather than fully replacing it.
