AI Class Project

Artificial Intelligence in Economics | Fall 2024

Project Overview

This project explores how artificial intelligence tools can be used to analyze economic data and support decision-making in business and policy contexts.

Using a combination of machine learning models and econometric techniques, the project examines patterns in real or simulated datasets and evaluates the predictive power of different approaches.

The goal is to demonstrate how AI methods can complement traditional economic analysis, highlight their limitations, and reflect on the ethical implications of data-driven decision-making.
